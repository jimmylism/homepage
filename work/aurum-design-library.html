13 — Disclosure

Accordion

The Gold Membership includes unlimited access to our curated portfolio of over 400 properties worldwide, a dedicated personal concierge available 24 hours a day, complimentary room upgrades subject to availability, and exclusive invitations to member-only events and previews.
Members may cancel or modify reservations up to 48 hours before arrival at no charge. For Obsidian tier members, this window extends to 24 hours. Cancellations within the window are subject to a one-night retention fee, waivable once per calendar year.
Memberships are personal and non-transferable. However, you may add up to two companion profiles to your account, allowing trusted guests to access member benefits when travelling independently. Contact your concierge advisor for details.
We accept all major currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, CHF, and AED. Membership fees are billed in your home currency at the prevailing interbank rate with no foreign exchange surcharge applied.
